# 1. Executive Summary

## Project Purpose
This system transforms IGCSE past paper questions into professional, engaging educational animations for social media distribution. The system must maintain strict academic accuracy while creating visually compelling content that demonstrates educational authority and attracts students and parents to T.Y. Supplementary Centre's tutoring services.

## Success Criteria
- **Academic Accuracy:** 100% alignment with IGCSE specifications and examiner expectations
- **Visual Quality:** Professional, sophisticated aesthetic that distinguishes content from generic tutoring materials
- **Engagement Metrics:** High completion rates, saves, and shares on TikTok and Facebook
- **Production Efficiency:** Scalable system that can generate multiple videos per week
- **Brand Positioning:** Content establishes T.Y. Supplementary Centre as IGCSE subject matter experts

## Target Audience
- **Primary:** IGCSE students (14-16 years) in English as Second Language (ESL) contexts
- **Secondary:** Parents evaluating tutoring services for their children
- **Tertiary:** Teachers seeking supplementary materials
